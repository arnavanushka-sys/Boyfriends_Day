HAPPY BOYFRIEND’S DAY

dear Arnav..

I don’t really know what to say now that everything between us is over. I thought if nothing else, I’d at least give you this gift finished. At least we’ll be able to say we finished what we started — that we gave it our all. After so many chances, it’s for sure now that we were never actually meant to be together.
You’ve done things that deeply hurt me, things I never imagined I’d experience at the hands of someone I loved so much. And still, I don’t hate you. I don’t know how to. My love for you didn’t disappear just because the relationship broke. But that doesn’t mean I can stay, either.
So if there’s anything left in you that ever cared for me, please — don’t respond to my messages again. If not that don’t come back, not even if I cry, not even if I beg. Even if my heart aches so loudly it drowns out everything else, please don’t let us fall into the same cycle. Let this be the final act of love from you: walk away, and don’t look back.
I gave you everything I had. Every part of me yet I was not enough. But I’ve no regrets, I gave my 1000%. I was real. I know now I deserve better — not someone else, but a better version of myself. I deserve to take all that love I gave to you and finally give it back to me. I owe that to the girl who kept trying.
I’ll always carry the warmth of our good memories, even if they’re wrapped in sadness now. And I’ll carry the pain too — the kind that makes me question if I’ll ever feel enough again. You’ve left me with pieces of myself I don’t quite recognize anymore.
But still, thank you. For the love, for the lessons, and for showing me the parts of me that still need healing. I just wish goodbye didn’t have to hurt this much.

- Your Mice<3
